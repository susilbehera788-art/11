# **App Name**: Bharat Cricket Chronicles

## Core Features:

- Homepage Dashboard: Display a clean hero banner with 'Indian Cricket Journey' and quick navigation buttons.
- Live Score Updates: Provide real-time scores and updates for ongoing cricket matches.
- Indian Cricketers Directory: List profiles of Indian cricketers with key stats and achievements.
- Trophies & History Showcase: Detail the trophies won by the Indian cricket team, along with significant historical moments.
- Current Players Roster: Maintain an updated list of current players in the Indian cricket team.
- Live Score Section: Display live match teams, current score, overs/run rate, wickets, and optional commentary. Sync with a cricket API or custom scraping service.
- Indian Cricket History Section: Showcase all trophies India has won (1970–present), including World Cups, ICC Trophies, and Asia Cup. Display a year-wise timeline with photos and short stories of each trophy.
- Indian Cricketers Section: Provide full profiles of each cricketer, including full name, debut year, retirement year, total matches, ODI/T20/Test runs & wickets, short life story, career achievements, and photos.
- Current Indian Players List: Show all active Indian players, their formats (Test, ODI, T20), recent match performances, basic stats, and upcoming matches.
- Footer Section: Display company details at the bottom of the website, including company name, mobile number, address, copyright notice, and contact email.
- Contact Page Map: Show company location directly using Google Maps iframe.
- Dashboard Map Option: A dedicated button in dashboard that opens the map with company location.
- Credit Card Apply Option: Add a “Credit Card Apply” option on every page. Clicking it redirects to WhatsApp (same number: +918480447800)
- Google AdSense Integration: Integrate Google AdSense by placing the AdSense script inside the <head> section and adding responsive ad units inside the homepage, news page, cricket page, and shopping page. Ensure ads automatically adjust in mobile and desktop view.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant India blue (#1388C7), evoking the national cricket team's identity and passion.
- Background color: Light gray (#E8F0FE), providing a clean and modern backdrop to accentuate the vibrant primary color.
- Accent color: Saffron (#FF9933), mirroring the Indian flag, and used for highlights and important actions.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font that conveys a modern, objective, and neutral feel; suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean and modern icons related to cricket, such as bats, balls, stadiums, and trophies.
- Employ a grid-based layout with clear sections for each category (Live Score, Cricketers, History, etc.).
- Subtle animations for loading new scores or transitioning between sections to enhance user engagement.
